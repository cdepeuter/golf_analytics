Weather Columns:

c("tempF", "humidity", "wDirDeg","wDir", "windGust","precip","rain", "conds", "weatherTime")

tempF - temperature in farenheit
humidity - % humidity
wDirDeg - wind direction in degrees
windDir - wind direcion desccription (SSE)
windGust - wind gust in mph
precip - precipitation in inches
conds - description of conditions
weatherTime - time of weather observation
